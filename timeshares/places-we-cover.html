How do Timeshares work?

  • A timeshare is a way for a number of people to share ownership of a property, usually a vacation property such as a condominium unit within a resort area.
  • Each buyer usually purchases a certain period of time in a particular unit. Timeshares typically divide the property into one- to two-week periods.
  • If a buyer desires a longer time period, purchasing several consecutive timeshares may be an option (if available).
